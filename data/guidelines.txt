[AQI:201-300]

Purifier: Required (1 per room / 2 per floor)

Actions:
- Avoid outdoor movement unless necessary
- Close windows during high pollution
- Avoid construction dust
Message: Air quality is very poor. Strong precautions are needed.

[AQI:301+]
Mask: N95 or KN95 mandatory
Purifier: Required in all living spaces
Checkup: Immediate consultation if symptoms appear
Actions:
- Stay indoors as much as possible
- Work from home if possible
- Follow government advisories
Message: Severe air pollution. Health protection is critical.
